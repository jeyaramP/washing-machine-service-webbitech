Common Washer Problems

Recognize these symptoms? Call us before minor issues become major failures.

Not Spinning

Check belt tension, inspect motor carbon brushes, or replace faulty lid switch.

Water Leakage

Replace damaged door seal/gasket, tighten inlet hoses, or fix drain pump cracks.

Loud Noise

Replace worn drum bearings, install new shock absorbers, or remove trapped coins.

Not Draining

Clean out blocked lint filters, clear drain hose blockages, or replace faulty drain pump.

Door Stuck

Faulty door interlock switch, broken handle, or water still left inside the drum.

Bad Odor

Mold and mildew buildup in the door seal or detergent drawer requiring deep cleaning.

Overfilling

Defective water inlet valve or faulty pressure switch causing the tub to overflow.

Excessive Shaking

Unbalanced load, unlevel feet, or broken shock absorbers and suspension springs.
